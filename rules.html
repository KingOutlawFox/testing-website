Community Guidelines

Preserving the immersion, respect, and enjoyment of our supernatural roleplay experience.

Fundamental Roleplay Principles

  1. Maintain consistent and immersive character roleplay at all times.
  2. Respect other players' roleplay experiences and storylines.
  3. Avoid out-of-character (OOC) communication during active roleplay scenarios.
  4. Character actions must be realistic and within the realm of your character's capabilities.

Combat and Conflict Resolution

  1. All combat must be consensual and follow the server's combat mechanics.
  2. Supernatural abilities must be balanced and approved by server administration.
  3. No meta-gaming (using out-of-character knowledge in character interactions).
  4. Respect the power dynamics and established lore of supernatural factions.

Community Conduct

  1. Treat all community members with respect and dignity.
  2. No harassment, discrimination, or offensive language will be tolerated.
  3. Constructive communication is key to maintaining a positive community environment.
  4. Staff decisions are final and must be respected.
Warning: Repeated violations of community guidelines may result in temporary or permanent server bans.

Character Development Guidelines

  1. Character backgrounds must be approved by server administration.
  2. Maintain character consistency and avoid sudden, unrealistic personality shifts.
  3. Progression of supernatural abilities must be gradual and narratively justified.
  4. Cross-server character continuity is subject to administrative review.

About Us

ModernNightsRP is committed to creating an immersive and respectful roleplay environment.
