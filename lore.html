Two Realms, One Story

Explore the supernatural world of Houston across two unique roleplay experiences: FiveM and RedM.

FiveM: Urban Supernatural Intrigue

In our FiveM server, players dive into a modern, urban supernatural setting where the supernatural world intersects with everyday life in Houston. This server focuses on:

  • Modern Supernatural Dynamics: Navigate complex social structures of vampire covens, werewolf packs, and magical orders.
  • City-Based Roleplay: Interact with various institutions, from local government to underground criminal networks.
  • Career Paths: Choose from a wide range of roles including law enforcement, criminal elements, supernatural investigators, and more.
  • Advanced Progression: Develop your character's skills, relationships, and supernatural abilities over time.
Two Unique Experiences

RedM: Wild West Supernatural Frontier

Our RedM server offers a historical supernatural experience set in the late 19th century Texas frontier. This server provides a different perspective on supernatural existence:

  • Historical Setting: Experience the supernatural world during the post-Civil War era, where mystical forces clash with expanding civilization.
  • Frontier Supernatural Conflicts: Explore tensions between Native American mystical traditions, European supernatural immigrants, and local magical ecosystems.
  • Survival and Adaptation: Navigate a harsher world where supernatural beings must fight for territory and survival.
  • Unique Character Archetypes: Play as frontier lawmen, Native American shamans, European supernatural immigrants, or local magical practitioners.

Interconnected Narratives

While FiveM and RedM offer distinct experiences, they share a connected supernatural universe. Events and legendary figures from the RedM era directly influence the modern-day supernatural politics of the FiveM server, creating a rich, layered narrative experience.
